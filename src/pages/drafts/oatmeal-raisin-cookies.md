## Ingredients

### Dry

- 1 1/2 c AP flour
- 3/4 c unsalted butter, room temp
- 2 eggs
- 1 c light brown sugar
- 1/2 c sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1 1/2 c raisins
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 c vegetable oil
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp clove
- 1/4 tsp allspice
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg (fresh ground)
- 2 c old fashioned oats

## Hardware

- stand mixer (optional)

## Instructions

350f
lined cookie sheets
beat butter oil sugars eggs vanilla
separate bowl, flour baking soda spices salt oats
fold dry into wet
then fold in raisins
1 1/2 tbsp portions
12 min oven (edges golden)
cool on tray 10min
move to wire rack
